## Features

- **Send securely** shares the selected files over a private link that's copied to your clipboard and expires on its own
    > Files transfer straight from your Mac, so a link only works while you're sharing it
- **Transfers** panel on the Send button shows each active link's download count, a live countdown to expiry, and lets you copy, reschedule, or stop it
- A notification tells you when someone finishes downloading a file you sent
- Send securely straight from the right-click menu, using your default expiration
- **Open With** and **Scripts** rows: one-tap pills, with app and script icons, to open or run the selected files
    > Each app is keyed by the first letter of its name; when several apps share a letter, that key opens a picker where `1`–`9` pick a row
- **Custom shortcuts**: rebind any toolbar action's keyboard shortcut in Settings, under Shortcuts
- **Toggle Preview** to show or hide the preview panel from the `⋯` menu or its own shortcut (`⌘⇧P` by default)
- Hold `⌘` to reveal every action's shortcut right on its button, or `⌘⌥` and `⌘⌃` for the Open With and Scripts rows

## Improvements

- **Redesigned action toolbar**: actions are grouped into sections with the less-used ones moved into a `⋯` menu, so the row stays clean and glanceable
- Restyle the toolbar to taste: icon-and-text, text-only, or icon-only buttons, regular or compact spacing, optional dividers and background, with clearer hover and press feedback
- Decide where each action lives, in the bar, the `⋯` menu, or hidden, from Settings
- The `⋯` menu shows each action's keyboard shortcut alongside it
- Send the selected files without touching the mouse: `⌘U`, then `Return`
- A first-run explainer covers how secure send works and that your Mac and Cling need to stay running while a link is live
- Recent transfers are capped at three, with a Clear action, and big multi-file sends are summarized instead of listing every name
- Settings appearance options move into a **Style** pane with segmented pickers and a dedicated window and Dock section
- The results table, preview panel, and action row corners match the window's rounding
- **License & updates** settings pane

## Fixes

- `Esc` closes the Send popover instead of hiding the whole window
